I've started a general-purpose robot company, [Genesis AI](https://genesis-ai.company). [Come build with us!](https://genesis-ai.company/join-us)

Before this, I was a research scientist at [Mistral AI](https://mistral.ai/), working on accelerating AI open-source, and did an ML PhD at Carnegie Mellon University, working on computer vision and robotics with [Katerina Fragkiadaki](https://www.cs.cmu.edu/~katef). 
I took a couple of years of leave to build the ML product & team of [Relyance AI](https://www.relyance.ai/).

I grew up in Paris, and my English accent has long been stuck at a local optimum.
I like good food and wine, traveling, nature, and lifting weights while listening to cool science or philosophy.
